Welcome to Calculator Application

An open source REST APIs repository for financial calculators



1. GET: /cal_app/inv_cal//salary/age

Proposes the annual investment details and gains based on Salary and Age.

Result:
 HighRisk: Amount to investing in high risk investments.
            
 MidRisk: Amount to investing in medium risk investments.
            
 LowRisk: Amount to investing in low risk investments.
            
 Invested_Amount: Actual Amount invested in 5 years.
            
 Estimated_Investment_Return: Estimated Return from investments as of current rate.
            
 Estimated_Bank_Return: Estimated Return if saved in Bank as of current rate.
            
 Investment_Gain: Estimated Gain from investments as of current rate.
            
 Bank_Gain: Estimated Gain from Bank savings as of current rate.


2. GET: /cal_app/curr_conv

Converts input amount into Indian Currency Equivalent.

Supported Currencies:
USD
            
CAD
            
AUD
            
EUR
            
JPY
            
GBP
            
RUB
            
SGD

Sample input - https://cal-app-osse-28.herokuapp.com/cal_app/curr_conv/100/USD


Sample output - {"Amount":100,"Currency":"USD","INR_Equivalent":7209.999999999999}
